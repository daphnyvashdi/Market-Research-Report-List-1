<p><ul><li>Diode Rectifier</li><li>Thyristor Rectifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power rectifiers market includes two primary types: diode rectifiers and thyristor rectifiers. Diode rectifiers are simple and efficient for converting alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC), commonly used in low to moderate power applications. In contrast, thyristor rectifiers offer enhanced control over output voltage and current, making them suitable for high-power applications like motor drives and industrial processes. Both types are essential for power management in various electronic and electrical systems.</p></p>
