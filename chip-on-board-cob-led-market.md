<p><strong>The Chip-on-board (CoB) LED Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Chip</li><li>Vertical Chip</li><li>Flip Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chip-on-Board (CoB) LED market comprises three primary types: lateral chip, vertical chip, and flip chip. Lateral chip technology arranges the LED chip horizontally, optimizing light output for applications like signage. Vertical chips are mounted upright, enhancing heat dissipation and efficiency, making them suitable for high-power applications. Flip chip technology flips the chip to connect it directly to the substrate, reducing thermal resistance and enabling compact designs, ideal for advanced lighting solutions and high-density applications.</p></p>
