<p><p>Ultraviolet Curing Systems encompass various technologies for curing adhesives, coatings, and inks. Mercury UV lamps are traditional and provide high-intensity UV light, suitable for fast curing but with environmental concerns. Metal halide lamps offer a broader spectrum and are effective for larger areas but have longer warm-up times. In contrast, LED curing systems are more energy-efficient, environmentally friendly, and generate less heat, allowing for immediate curing with a longer lifespan. Each type serves specific applications depending on energy efficiency, curing speed, and ecological considerations.</p></p>
