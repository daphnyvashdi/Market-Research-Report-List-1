<p><p>The polyurethane flexible foams market comprises two primary types: polyether and polyester foams. Polyether foams are known for their good resilience, hydrolytic stability, and lower cost, making them ideal for applications in furniture, bedding, and automotive seating. In contrast, polyester foams offer superior durability, higher tensile strength, and better resistance to chemicals and UV exposure, making them suitable for industrial uses, automotive interiors, and decorative applications. Each type serves distinct market needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
