<p><p>Detonation flame arresters are safety devices used to prevent the propagation of flame in explosive atmospheres. The market can be segmented into two main types: In-line and End-of-line. In-line detonation arresters are installed within a pipeline, designed to stop flames from traveling upstream in a flow system. Conversely, end-of-line arresters are placed at the termination point of a pipeline, preventing flame travel into the system. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety in industrial environments handling flammable gases.</p></p>
