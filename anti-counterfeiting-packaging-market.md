<p><p>The anti-counterfeiting packaging market comprises two main types: authentication packaging technology and track and trace packaging technology. Authentication packaging involves methods like holograms, QR codes, and unique serial numbers to verify product legitimacy at the consumer level. Track and trace packaging utilizes serialization and advanced tracking systems to monitor and trace products through the supply chain, enabling the detection of counterfeiting and ensuring product integrity. Both technologies enhance consumer trust and enable companies to protect their brands effectively.</p></p>
