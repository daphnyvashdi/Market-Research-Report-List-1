<p><ul><li>Tadpole Style</li><li>Delta Style</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recumbent tricycle market is primarily divided into two styles: tadpole and delta. Tadpole-style tricycles feature two front wheels and one rear wheel, offering superior stability and aerodynamics, making them popular for speed and performance. In contrast, delta-style tricycles have one front wheel and two rear wheels, providing easier handling and often better visibility. Each design caters to different user needs, appealing to commuter cyclists, recreational riders, and those seeking ergonomic benefits while cycling.</p></p>
