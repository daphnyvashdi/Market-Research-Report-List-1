<p><p>The pressure fryer market is primarily divided into two types: electric pressure fryers and gas pressure fryers. Electric pressure fryers use electrical energy to heat and cook food under pressure, offering precise temperature control and ease of use, making them ideal for commercial kitchens with limited ventilation. Gas pressure fryers, on the other hand, utilize gas as a fuel source, providing faster heating and higher output capacity, suitable for high-demand settings. Both types enhance cooking efficiency and improve flavor retention in fried foods.</p></p>
