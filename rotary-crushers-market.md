<p><p>The rotary crushers market is divided into two main types: single cavity rotary crushers and double cavity rotary crushers. Single cavity rotary crushers feature a singular crushing chamber designed for specific material sizes, providing a more compact solution. In contrast, double cavity rotary crushers have two interconnected cavities, allowing for higher throughput and versatility in processing various materials. The choice between these types depends on the required output, material characteristics, and operational efficiency in industrial applications.</p></p>
