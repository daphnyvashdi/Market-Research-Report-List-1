<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Portable MRI Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Superconducting-magnet-based Portable MRI</li><li>Electromagnet-based Portable MRI</li><li>Permanent-magnet-based Portable MRI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable MRI market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Superconducting-magnet-based Portable MRI** utilizes superconducting magnets for high-resolution imaging, requiring cooling systems but offering superior image quality. </p><p>2. **Electromagnet-based Portable MRI** employs electromagnets for a lightweight design, providing moderate image quality and flexibility in different settings. </p><p>3. **Permanent-magnet-based Portable MRI** relies on permanent magnets, ensuring energy efficiency and reduced operational costs, though typically with lower image clarity compared to superconducting models. Each type caters to varying needs based on imaging requirements and operational constraints.</p></p>
