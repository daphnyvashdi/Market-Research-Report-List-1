The market is segmented into two primary types: surface preparation and pipe lining. Surface preparation includes cleaning and treating the pipe surfaces to ensure optimal adhesion of coatings, while pipe lining is the application of protective liners inside the pipes to enhance flow efficiency and extend lifespan. Both services are critical for maintaining pipeline integrity in various industries, including oil, gas, and water management.</p></p>
