<p><p>Roller Compacted Concrete (RCC) Dams are a type of gravity dam constructed using a method that involves the use of a dry concrete mix, which is compacted using heavy rollers. This technique offers significant advantages in cost, speed of construction, and material efficiency compared to traditional concrete dam construction methods. The RCC dam construction process reduces water consumption and allows for rapid placement, making it increasingly popular for hydropower projects and flood control applications.</p><p>The Roller Compacted Concrete Dam Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.2% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Roller Compacted Concrete Dam market primarily focuses on two applications: Massive Head Buttress Dams and Arch Dams.
