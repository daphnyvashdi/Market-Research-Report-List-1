<p><p>The Animal Health Diagnosis market encompasses various diagnostic types crucial for veterinary care. Clinical biochemistry involves analyzing blood samples to assess organ function and metabolic state. Urinalysis examines urine to detect diseases and monitor health. Immunodiagnostics utilize antigen-antibody interactions for disease detection, focusing on infectious and autoimmune disorders. Hematology tests evaluate blood components to diagnose conditions like anemia. Molecular diagnostics use DNA/RNA technologies for precise pathogen identification, providing critical insights into infectious diseases and genetic disorders in animals.</p></p>
