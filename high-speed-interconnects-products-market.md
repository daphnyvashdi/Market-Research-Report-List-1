<p><p>The high-speed interconnects products market encompasses various connectivity solutions, primarily focusing on Active Optical Cables (AOC) and Direct Attach Cables (DAC). AOCs use fiber optics and transmit data over longer distances with low power consumption, making them ideal for data centers and high-bandwidth applications. DACs, utilizing copper wiring, are more cost-effective for short-range connections, offering reliable data transmission in compact environments. Both types cater to the increasing demand for faster, efficient data transfers in modern networking infrastructures.</p></p>
