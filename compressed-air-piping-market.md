<p><p>The compressed air piping market comprises three main types: aluminum, steel, and plastic. Aluminum pipes are lightweight, resistant to corrosion, and offer excellent thermal efficiency, making them ideal for diverse applications. Steel pipes, known for their strength and durability, are commonly used in high-pressure systems; however, they may require protective coatings to prevent rust. Plastic pipes, often made of PVC or polyethylene, are lightweight, cost-effective, and resistant to corrosion but are generally suited for lower pressure applications. Each type serves unique operational needs.</p></p>
