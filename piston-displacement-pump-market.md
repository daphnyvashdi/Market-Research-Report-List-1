<p><p>The piston displacement pump market comprises several types, including lift pumps, force pumps, axial piston pumps, and radial piston pumps. Lift pumps use piston action to raise fluids, while force pumps generate pressure to push fluid through a system. Axial piston pumps feature pistons aligned parallel to the drive shaft, ideal for high-pressure applications, whereas radial piston pumps arrange pistons in a circular pattern, offering efficient fluid transfer with high torque. Together, these pumps serve diverse industrial needs in fluid management.</p></p>
