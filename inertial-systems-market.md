<p><p>The inertial systems market encompasses various technologies, including Attitude Heading Reference Systems (AHRS), Inertial Positioning and Orientation Systems, and Inertial Measurement Units (IMU). AHRS provide critical orientation data using gyroscopes and accelerometers, essential for navigation in aerospace and marine applications. Inertial Positioning and Orientation Systems focus on calculating precise positioning based on movement data. IMUs combine accelerometers and gyroscopes to measure acceleration and angular velocity, serving diverse sectors such as automotive, robotics, and consumer electronics for motion tracking and stabilization.</p></p>
