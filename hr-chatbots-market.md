<p><p>HR chatbots can be categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise chatbots are installed locally within an organization's infrastructure, offering greater control and security but requiring significant maintenance and IT resources. In contrast, cloud-based chatbots are hosted on remote servers, providing easier scalability, flexibility, and lower upfront costs. These solutions allow organizations to quickly implement and update chatbot capabilities without the constraints of physical hardware, making them a popular choice for many businesses.</p></p>
