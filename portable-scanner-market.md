<p><ul><li>Laser Scanners</li><li>Linear Imager</li><li>2D Imager</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable scanner market primarily comprises three types: laser scanners, linear imagers, and 2D imagers. Laser scanners utilize laser beams to capture detailed and precise barcodes, making them efficient for high-speed scanning. Linear imagers rely on a single line of light to read barcodes, offering good performance in various lighting conditions. In contrast, 2D imagers scan barcodes in two dimensions, enabling the reading of complex barcodes and QR codes, thereby enhancing versatility and functionality in diverse applications.</p></p>
