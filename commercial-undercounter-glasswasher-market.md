<p><p>The Commercial Undercounter Glasswasher market includes two main types: Gravity Waste Type and Pumped Waste Type. Gravity Waste Type models rely on natural drainage, using gravity to remove wastewater, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. Pumped Waste Type models use a pump to expel wastewater, offering greater flexibility in installation, especially where drainage points are not at floor level. Both types cater to small to medium-sized establishments, providing efficient glass cleaning with compact designs suitable for undercounter use.</p></p>
