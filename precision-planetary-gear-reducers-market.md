<p><ul><li>Linear Precision Planetary Gearbox</li><li>Right Angle Precision Planetary Gearbox</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precision planetary gear reducers are categorized into two main types: linear precision planetary gearboxes and right angle precision planetary gearboxes. Linear precision planetary gearboxes provide a direct, inline power transmission with high efficiency and minimal backlash, ideal for applications requiring linear motion and precise control. In contrast, right angle precision planetary gearboxes offer a 90-degree power transmission, making them suitable for space-constrained applications where the input and output shafts are perpendicular, while still delivering high torque and accuracy.</p></p>
