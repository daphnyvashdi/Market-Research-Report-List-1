<p><p>A Rubber Tyred Gantry (RTG) Crane is a large mobile crane designed primarily for container handling in ports and intermodal facilities. These cranes enable efficient loading, unloading, and stacking of containers, significantly improving productivity in logistics and transportation sectors.</p><p>The Rubber Tyred Gantry Crane Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.1% during the forecast period.
