<p><ul><li>2-Way Air Operated Valves</li><li>3-Way Air Operated Valves</li><li>4-Way Air Operated Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air-operated valves market comprises various types, including 2-way, 3-way, and 4-way valves. 2-way air-operated valves control the flow of air or fluids in a single direction, making them ideal for on/off applications. 3-way air-operated valves can divert flow between two outlets, allowing for more complex routing of fluids. 4-way air-operated valves offer even greater versatility by enabling flow control between multiple ports, commonly used in pneumatic systems for tasks like actuating cylinders or controlling air direction.</p></p>
