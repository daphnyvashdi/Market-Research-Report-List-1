<p><p>CO2 skin laser scanning machines are categorized into three main types: Super Pulse, Continuous Wave, and Other. Super Pulse lasers deliver short bursts of high-energy light, allowing for precise skin resurfacing with minimal thermal damage. Continuous Wave lasers provide a steady emission of energy, effective for deeper tissue penetration and more aggressive treatments. Other market types may include fractional and hybrid technologies, which combine features of both Super Pulse and Continuous Wave to enhance treatment versatility and patient comfort.</p></p>
