<p><p>The electric vehicle (EV) battery market primarily features Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4) batteries and Three Element Lithium batteries. In contrast, Three Element Lithium batteries incorporate lithium nickel cobalt manganese oxide, offering higher energy density, which enhances vehicle range and performance.
