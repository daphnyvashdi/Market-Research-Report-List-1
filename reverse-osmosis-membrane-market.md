<p><strong>The Reverse Osmosis Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cellulose-Based Membranes</li><li>Thin Film Composite Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reverse osmosis membrane market primarily features two types: cellulose-based membranes and thin film composite membranes. Cellulose-based membranes are natural and cost-effective, often used in low-pressure applications but have limited durability and fouling resistance. In contrast, thin film composite membranes are synthetic and offer superior performance, higher rejection rates, and enhanced durability, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including seawater desalination and water purification. These distinct characteristics drive their adoption in various industries.</p></p>
