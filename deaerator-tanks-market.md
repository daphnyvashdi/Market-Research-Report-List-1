<p><p>The deaerator tanks market is primarily segmented into tray-type, spray-type, and other types. Spray-type deaerator tanks, on the other hand, employ a spray mechanism to achieve similar results by dispersing water into fine droplets for better gas elimination. Other types may include hybrid designs or specialized systems tailored for unique industrial applications, each contributing to effective deaeration processes.</p></p>
