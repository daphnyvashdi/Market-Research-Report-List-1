<p><p>The capacitive inclinometer market is categorized into single axis and dual axis types. Single axis capacitive inclinometers measure tilt or inclination in one direction, making them ideal for applications requiring simple angle measurements. In contrast, dual axis capacitive inclinometers measure tilt in both horizontal and vertical planes, providing comprehensive data for complex applications. These inclinometers are widely used in civil engineering, construction, and aerospace for monitoring structural stability and ensuring precision in alignment and positioning tasks.</p></p>
