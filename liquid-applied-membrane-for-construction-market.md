<p><strong>The Liquid Applied Membrane for Construction Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bituminous</li><li>Elastomeric</li><li>Cementitious</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid Applied Membranes (LAM) in the construction market are categorized into three main types: Bituminous, Elastomeric, and Cementitious. </p><p>Bituminous membranes are petroleum-based and offer excellent waterproofing and UV resistance, commonly used in roofing systems. Elastomeric membranes, made from polymers, provide flexibility and durability, ideal for areas with temperature fluctuations. Cementitious membranes, composed of cement and additives, provide a rigid waterproof barrier suitable for various substrates, particularly in below-grade applications. Each type caters to specific environmental conditions and project requirements.</p></p>
