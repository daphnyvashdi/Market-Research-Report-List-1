<p><p>The electric valve market encompasses various types, prominently featuring electric ball valves and electric butterfly valves. Electric ball valves offer precise flow control with minimal pressure drop, suitable for on/off operations in various industries. In contrast, electric butterfly valves provide efficient regulation of flow and pressure, ideal for applications requiring quick actuation and large volume control. Both valve types enhance automation and remote control capabilities in processes like water treatment, HVAC systems, and industrial applications, driving their growing adoption.</p></p>
