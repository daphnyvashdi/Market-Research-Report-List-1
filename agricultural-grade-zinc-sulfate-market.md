<p><p>The Agricultural Grade Zinc Sulfate market primarily features two types: Zinc Sulfate Monohydrate and Zinc Sulfate Heptahydrate. Zinc Sulfate Monohydrate contains one molecule of water and is favored for its higher nutrient concentration, making it effective as a soil amendment and micronutrient source. In contrast, Zinc Sulfate Heptahydrate, with seven water molecules, is often used for its solubility and effectiveness in fertilization, especially in liquid applications. Both forms enhance crop yield and quality by addressing zinc deficiency in soils.</p></p>
