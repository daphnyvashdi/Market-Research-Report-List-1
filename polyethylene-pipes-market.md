MDPE pipes, with medium density, are used for gas supply. PEX pipes are flexible and temperature-resistant, commonly used in plumbing. LDPE pipes are lightweight and flexible, often utilized in irrigation systems. Together, these variations meet diverse industry requirements.</p></p>
