<p><p>The Aerospace Electromechanical Switch market encompasses various types of switches, each serving distinct functions in avionics and control systems. Tactile switches provide feedback upon activation, while rotary switches enable multi-position control. Encoders convert movement into electrical signals, and toggle switches allow manual operation. Push switches facilitate momentary activation, while detect switches serve as sensors. Micro switches are compact and precise, and DIP switches are customizable for various settings. Additional types include power, slide, and joystick switches, enhancing operational versatility.</p></p>
