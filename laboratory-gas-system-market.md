<p><p>The Laboratory Gas System market comprises two main types: Semi-Automatic Switchover Systems and Fully Automatic Switchover Systems. Semi-Automatic systems require manual intervention to switch gas sources when one depletes, allowing for cost-effective and simpler setups. In contrast, Fully Automatic systems autonomously manage gas flow and changeovers, enhancing efficiency and minimizing downtime. Both systems are essential for laboratories needing reliable gas supply for various applications, with the choice depending on operational needs and budget considerations.</p></p>
