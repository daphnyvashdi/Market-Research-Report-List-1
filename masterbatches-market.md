<p><ul><li>White Masterbatch</li><li>Black Masterbatch</li><li>Color Masterbatch</li><li>Additive Masterbatch</li><li>Plastic Filler Masterbatch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The masterbatch market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. White masterbatch enhances opacity and brightness in plastics, while black masterbatch provides color depth and UV protection. Color masterbatch incorporates vibrant pigments into polymers for diverse hues. Additive masterbatch includes functional additives like stabilizers, antioxidants, and flame retardants to enhance material performance. Plastic filler masterbatch uses fillers to reduce material costs while maintaining desired properties. Together, these masterbatches serve critical roles in the production and quality of plastic products.</p></p>
