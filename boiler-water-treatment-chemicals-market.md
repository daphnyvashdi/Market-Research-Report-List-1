<p><p>The boiler water treatment chemicals market consists of several key types, each playing a critical role in maintaining boiler efficiency and longevity. Corrosion inhibitors prevent damage to metal surfaces caused by corrosive elements. Scale inhibitors reduce the formation of mineral deposits that can hinder heat transfer. Oxygen scavengers eliminate dissolved oxygen, minimizing the risk of pitting and corrosion. Other chemicals may include pH adjusters and biocides, which help maintain optimal water conditions and prevent microbial growth, ensuring the overall effectiveness of the treatment process.</p></p>
