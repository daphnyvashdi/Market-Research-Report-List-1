<p><strong>The Hatchback Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting</li><li>Forging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hatchback wheel market primarily consists of two manufacturing types: casting and forging. Casting involves pouring molten metal into molds, allowing for complex shapes and designs at lower costs, making it a preferred choice for mass production. In contrast, forging entails shaping metal through high pressure and temperature, resulting in stronger, more durable wheels. While casting is often used for everyday hatchbacks, forging is favored in performance-oriented models due to its superior structural integrity and weight reduction benefits.</p></p>
