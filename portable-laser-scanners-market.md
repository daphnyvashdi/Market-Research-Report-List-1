<p><p>Portable laser scanners are classified into four main types based on their design and application. Handheld scanners offer flexibility and mobility for capturing data in tight spaces. Tripod-mounted scanners provide stability and precision for larger areas, ideal for architectural and construction surveys. Automated and CMM-based scanners integrate with coordinate measuring machines for high-accuracy applications, often used in manufacturing. Desktop and stationary scanners are used for detailed scanning in controlled environments, suitable for smaller objects or intricate designs, ensuring high-resolution output.</p></p>
