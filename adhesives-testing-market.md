<p><strong>The Adhesives Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Peel Strength</li><li>Tack Strength</li><li>Shear Strength</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adhesives testing market focuses on evaluating the performance of adhesive materials through various strength metrics. Peel strength measures the force required to separate two bonded surfaces, indicating durability under tension. Tack strength assesses the initial stickiness of an adhesive, reflecting its ability to bond quickly without pressure. Shear strength evaluates the adhesive's resistance to sliding forces acting parallel to the bond, crucial for applications requiring stability under load. Together, these metrics ensure optimal adhesive performance in diverse applications.</p></p>
