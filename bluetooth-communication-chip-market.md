<p><strong>The Bluetooth Communication Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Dual Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bluetooth communication chip market can be categorized into two main types: Single Mode and Dual Mode. Single Mode chips strictly support Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), ideal for power-sensitive applications like wearables and IoT devices. In contrast, Dual Mode chips can handle both Bluetooth Classic and BLE, offering flexibility for devices needing higher data rates and compatibility with various Bluetooth versions. This differentiation allows manufacturers to select suitable chips based on performance requirements and application needs.</p></p>
