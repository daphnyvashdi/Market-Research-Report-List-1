<p><p>The industrial silicon market is categorized into various series based on aluminum alloy compositions. The 1xxx series contains pure aluminum (99% or higher) known for excellent corrosion resistance and conductivity. The 2xxx series includes copper-alloyed aluminum, recognized for high strength and machinability. The 3xxx series, with manganese as the primary alloying element, offers good workability and moderate strength. The 4xxx series features silicon, enhancing fluidity and lowering melting points for welding applications. Lastly, the 5xxx series incorporates magnesium, providing excellent corrosion resistance and weldability.</p></p>
