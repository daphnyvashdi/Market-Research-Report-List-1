<p><strong>The Graphite-based Forging Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Forging Lubricants</li><li>Cold Forging Lubricants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphite-based forging lubricants are essential in metalworking processes to reduce friction and wear. The market is divided into two main types: hot forging lubricants and cold forging lubricants. Hot forging lubricants are used at elevated temperatures, enhancing material flow and surface finish during high-temperature deformation. Cold forging lubricants, on the other hand, are designed for lower temperature applications, providing effective lubrication and protection against galling while maintaining dimensional accuracy. Both types play a critical role in optimizing forge efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
