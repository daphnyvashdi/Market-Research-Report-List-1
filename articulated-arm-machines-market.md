<p><p>The articulated arm machines market is divided into two main types: pneumatic and electric articulated arm machines. Pneumatic articulated arm machines leverage compressed air to power their movements, offering high flexibility and lightweight design, making them suitable for dynamic environments. Conversely, electric articulated arm machines use electric motors for precise control and are often favored for applications requiring accuracy and stability. Both types are utilized across various industries for tasks such as assembly, welding, and material handling.</p></p>
