<p><p>FFP masks are classified into three main types based on their filtration efficiency and intended use. FFP1 masks offer minimal protection and filter at least 80% of airborne particles, suitable for low-risk environments. FFP2 masks provide a higher level of protection by filtering at least 94% of particles, making them ideal for moderate-risk settings. FFP3 masks offer the highest filtration efficiency, capturing at least 99% of particles, and are designed for high-risk environments, such as medical or hazardous industries.</p></p>
