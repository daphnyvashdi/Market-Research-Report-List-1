<p><p>The prestressed steel strand market includes various types based on the filling material and application. Bare PC strand is uncoated and primarily used in environments requiring minimal corrosion protection. Grease-filled PC strand is coated with grease to enhance corrosion resistance and is suitable for harsher conditions. Wax-filled PC strand uses wax for moisture protection, providing a balance between performance and cost. Other types may include specialty strands designed for unique applications, catering to specific industry requirements. Together, these types address diverse construction and engineering needs.</p></p>
