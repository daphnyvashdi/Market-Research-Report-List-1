Second-trimester screening tests, performed between 15 and 20 weeks, include blood tests and ultrasound to detect developmental issues. Third-trimester screening tests focus on assessing fetal well-being and developmental progression. Diagnostic tests, such as amniocentesis and chorionic villus sampling, provide definitive results regarding genetic conditions.</p></p>
